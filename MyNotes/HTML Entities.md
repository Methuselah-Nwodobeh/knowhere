3. `&gt;` - Greater than (>)
4. `&quot;` - Quotation mark (")
5. `&apos;` - Apostrophe (')
6. `&nbsp;` - Non-breaking space ( )
7. `&reg;` - Registered trademark symbol (®)
8. `&trade;` - Trademark symbol (™)
9. `&cent;` - Cent symbol (¢)
10. `&pound;` - Pound sterling symbol (£)
11. `&euro;` - Euro symbol (€)
12. `&yen;` - Yen symbol (¥)
13. `&sect;` - Section symbol (§)
14. `&mdash;` - Em dash (—)
15. `&ndash;` - En dash (–)
